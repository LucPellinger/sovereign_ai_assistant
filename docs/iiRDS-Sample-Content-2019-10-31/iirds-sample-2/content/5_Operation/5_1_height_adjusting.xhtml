Adjusting the height

The height adjustment is infinitely variable.

  1. Hold the functional unit 1 by the handle at the top.
  2. With the other hand loosen the retaining screw 2 at the rear of the telescopic rod.
  3. Pull the functional unit 1 to the desired height.
  4. Tighten the retaining screw 2 by hand again hand tight.
  5. Release the handle.
The device is now at the desired height.
