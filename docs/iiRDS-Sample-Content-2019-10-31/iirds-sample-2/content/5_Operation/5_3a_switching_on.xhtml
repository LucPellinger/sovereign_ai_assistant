Switching on / Adjusting the speed

The device has 5 fan speed steps.

Use the rotary knob 1 to control the speed of the fan impeller.

  • Turn the knob one step to the right (clockwise) when the device is off, to turn it on. The fan will now rotate at the lowest speed.

  • Turn the knob one step to the right, when the device is turned on to increase the speed.

  • Turn the knob one step to the left (counter clockwise) to reduce the speed.

  • Turn the knob completely to the left (counter clockwise) to turn off the device.
The device is now deactivated and is not consuming any power.
