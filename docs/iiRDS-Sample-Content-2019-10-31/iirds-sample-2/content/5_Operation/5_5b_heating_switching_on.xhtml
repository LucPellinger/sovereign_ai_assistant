Switching the heating ON/OFF

CAUTION:

  • Danger of burns
  • Position the device at least 2 m away from people and heat-sensitive objects to avoid burns or damage.

You can switch the heating function of the device ON and OFF and set the heat level using slider 1.

  1. Move slider 1 to the left to adjust the heating to a low level.
    • The heater is now switched to a low level.
    • The single red LED lights up.
  2. Move slider 1 to the right to switch the heating to a higher level.
    • The heater is now switched to the higher level.
    • The double red LEDs light up.
  3. Move slider 1 to the middle to turn the heating OFF.
    • The heater is now switched off.
    • No red LEDs light up.
