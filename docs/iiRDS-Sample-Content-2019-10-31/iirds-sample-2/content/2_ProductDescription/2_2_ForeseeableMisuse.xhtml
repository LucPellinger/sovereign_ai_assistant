Foreseeable misuse

Observe the following instructions to avoid a foreseeable misuse:

  • Place the device for airing damp clothing at a minimum distance of 2 metres, and secure the garments with clothes pegs.
  • When transporting the device in containers other than the original packaging secure it against falling over and impact damage.
  • Do not make any structural changes to the unit which are not described in the manual.
  • Do not load or burden the device with other objects.
  • Do not insert objects or parts of the body through the safety grille, while the device is connected to the mains power supply.
  • Only use the device with a humidity of less than 65%.
  • Do not use the device for heating objects.
  • Place the device for heating the air at a minimum distance of 2 metres to persons and objects and turn on the swivel function on.
