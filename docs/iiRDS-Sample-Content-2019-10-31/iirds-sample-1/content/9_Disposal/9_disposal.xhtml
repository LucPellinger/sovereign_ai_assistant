Disposal

The device is subject to the requirements on disposal of electrical devices. For disposal of the device there are two options open to you:

Option 1

Dispose of the component parts of the device yourself in the containers provided for this purpose.

Component / Assembly Designated container
Metal parts Special waste
Plastic parts Recycling container
Packaging film Recycling container
Cardboard packaging Paper waste bin

Option 2

Hand over the device to your electrical appliance dealer or the municipal recycling point for electrical devices. They are obligated under the Electrical and Electronic Equipment Act (ElektroG) to take back (small) electrical devices.
