Switching the swivel function ON/OFF

CAUTION:

  • Risk of injury due to falling objects
  • Remove hindering objects from the vicinity of the device to prevent injury from falling objects.

Note:

  • Impairment to the device function due to obstructions
  • Remove hindering objects from the vicinity of the device to ensure the swivel function is free to move.

You can switch the swivel function of the device ON and OFF with button 1.

  1. Press button 1 until it audibly clicks, and then release it to turn the swivel function on.
    • The swivel function is now switched on and button 1 remains pressed down.
    • The green LED lights up.
  2. Press button 1 again, until it clicks, and then release it to turn the swivel function OFF.
    • The swivel function is now off and button 1 is back in its original position.
    • The green LED is off.
