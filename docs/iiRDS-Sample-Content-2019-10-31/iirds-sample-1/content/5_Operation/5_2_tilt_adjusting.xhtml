Adjusting the tilt

The fan has a tilt angle of -15° to +35°. You can adjust this tilt in steps of 5°.

  1. Hold the fan by the handle at the top.
  2. Tilt the fan until it is at the desired angle of tilt.

    Note:

    A clearly audible click indicates that the tilt angle has changed by one step.
