General safety instructions

When handling the device, observe the following safety instructions:

Warning:

  • Risk of electrical shock
  • Protect the device from moisture and rain.
  • Only make changes to the device, which are specifically described in the operating manual.

Warning:

  • Risk of fire
  • Keep the device away from readily combustible materials during operation.

CAUTION:

  • Risk of injury from falling device
  • Only place the device on flat surfaces to avoid injury to persons and pets, as well as damage to property.
  • Keep small children and pets away from the device.

Note:

  • Transport damage
  • For transport over long distances, use the complete packaging supplied to avoid damage to the device.
