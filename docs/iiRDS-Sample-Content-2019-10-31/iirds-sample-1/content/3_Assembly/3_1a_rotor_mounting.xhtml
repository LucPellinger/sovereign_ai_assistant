Mounting the rotor

CAUTION:

  • Small children or pets may swallow small parts
  • Keep small children and pets away from the place of assembly of the device to avoid small parts such as screws being swallowed.

  1. Slide the rear safety grille 1 onto the drive 2, as shown in Figure 1.
  2. Attach the rear safety grille 1 with the fastening nut 3 by screwing them hand tight with a crosshead screwdriver.

    The rear guard grill is now mounted.

  1. Push the fastening screw 4 through the rotor 5.
  2. Now push the fastening screw 4 into the screw fitting 3 and tighten them hand tight with a crosshead screwdriver.
    The rotor is now mounted.

  1. Place the front safety grille 6 on the rear safety grille 1, which you have already mounted, as shown in Figure 3.
  2. Fix the outer rings of the two grille halves together by pushing on the fixing clips evenly distributed over the two adjacent rings of the safety grille halves.
    The front guard grill is now mounted.

You have now finished mounting the rotor and the safety grille.
