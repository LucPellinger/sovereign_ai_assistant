Mounting the telescopic rod and base plate

  1. Fix the adjustment screw 1 onto the telescopic rod 2 as shown in Figure 1 and tighten the adjusting screw hand tight.
  2. Insert the telescopic rod 2 into the round opening on the top of the base 3.
  3. Attach the telescopic rod 2 to the base 3 by inserting the fixing screw 4 through the base, and tightening hand tight.
    The telescopic rod is now attached to the base.

  1. Take the functional unit 5, on which you have already installed the rotor and the safety grille, and push it onto the telescopic rod, as shown in Figure 2.
  2. Turn the functional unit 5 on the telescopic rod 2 until the adjustment screw 1 is at the back and the holes on the telescopic rod and the rear of the functional unit are lined up.
  3. Attach the functional unit 5 to the telescopic rod 2 by screwing the fixing screw 6 through the back of the control unit and the telescopic rod and tightening hand tight.
    The functional unit is now mounted on the telescopic rod.
You have now fully assembled the device and can start using it.
