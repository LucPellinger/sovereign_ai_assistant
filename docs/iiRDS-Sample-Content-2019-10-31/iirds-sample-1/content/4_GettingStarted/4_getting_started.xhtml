Getting started

The device is operated with a mains power voltage of 230V. Connect the device to the mains as described below.

  1. Make sure that the plug (A) and the plug socket, which you wish to use to operate the device, are not damaged.
  2. Connect the plug (A) to the socket (B).
The device is now ready to use.
