Cleaning the rotor

If the impeller of the rotor is dirty, you can clean it as follows.

  1. Disconnect the device from the mains.
  2. Remove the safety grille.
  3. Clean the rotor with a damp cloth.
  4. Mount the safety grille.
  5. Place the device back upright.
  6. Connect the device to the mains (see Getting started).
The rotor is now cleaned and the device is ready for use again.
