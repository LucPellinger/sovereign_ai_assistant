Clearing the swivel area

If the swivel function is switched on, it will turn off if there are objects located in the swivel area. For safety reasons the rotor is then also turned off. Once all hindering objects are removed, the rotor will immediately turn on again and the swivel function turns on again after 5 seconds.

In the event of a swivel function malfunction, please follow these steps:

  1. Remove all objects within a radius of 50cm around the device.
  2. Remove objects that hang from the ceiling into the swivel area.
  3. If there are no objects touching the device, check the power supply.
  4. If after performing the steps above you are still having problems with the swivel function, please contact your dealer.
