Checking the power supply

  1. Make sure that the power plug is fully inserted into the plug socket.
  2. If you operate the device on an extension socket or an extension cord, make sure that the extension socket or extension cord has been fully inserted into the wall socket and switched on.
  3. Plug another electrical device into the outlet used, and turn it on a trial basis to test the function of the socket.
